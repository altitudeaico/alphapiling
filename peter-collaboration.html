2. First Project: Alpha Piling

Project Overview

Build an end-to-end automation system for a piling contractor that:

  1. Monitors Gmail inbox for tender enquiries
  2. Downloads files from multiple sources (email, Dropbox, Dalux)
  3. Classifies documents and extracts pile schedule data
  4. Generates structured Excel output
  5. Organises files into Google Drive folder structure
  6. Sends acknowledgement email to sender

Scope clarity: The project will be delivered based on the agreed scope outlined above. Any additional requirements or changes must be agreed in writing by both parties and may impact cost and timeline.

Proposed Split

  • Bolaji: 50% (£2,750)

Split reflects: Peter handles build + delivery; Bolaji sourced client, manages relationship, provides specs/POC assets.

Timeline

  • Quoted to client: 8 weeks (buffer for scope creep)
  • Expected delivery: ~4 weeks if sample data received promptly
  • Client status: Interested — awaiting formal go-ahead and deposit

Timeline dependency: Delivery timelines are dependent on timely provision of data, access, and feedback from the client. Any delays on the client side will result in a corresponding adjustment to delivery dates.

9. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all information shared during this collaboration.

Confidential Information Includes

  • Client contact details, project requirements, and business information
  • Pricing structures, fee arrangements, and commercial terms
  • Lead sources, sales pipelines, and business development strategies
  • Technical approaches, proprietary tools, and system architectures
  • Internal business processes, financial information, and growth plans
  • Any information explicitly marked as confidential

Obligations

  • Non-disclosure: Neither party shall disclose confidential information to third parties without prior written consent
  • Limited use: Confidential information shall only be used for the purpose of this collaboration
  • Protection: Both parties shall take reasonable measures to protect confidential information from unauthorised access
  • Return/destruction: Upon termination, each party shall return or destroy confidential materials upon request

Duration: Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this agreement and remain in effect for a period of 3 years following the end of the collaboration.

10. Non-Solicitation & Non-Compete

To protect the business interests of both parties, the following restrictions apply:

Client Non-Solicitation

Restricted Activities

During this collaboration and for a period of 24 months following its termination, neither party shall:

  1. Direct solicitation: Directly contact, solicit, or approach any client introduced by the other party for the purpose of providing competing services outside of this collaboration
  2. Indirect solicitation: Use any third party, agent, or intermediary to approach such clients
  3. Diversion: Encourage or induce any client to reduce or terminate their business relationship with the other party
  4. Competitive engagement: Accept work from a client introduced by the other party without written consent

Client Ownership

  • Introducing party: The party who sources and introduces a client retains "ownership" of that client relationship
  • Alpha Piling: Alpha Piling Ltd is an Altitude AI client — introduced by Bolaji Olatoye
  • Future clients: Client ownership follows whoever makes the initial introduction and commercial relationship
  • Documentation: Both parties shall maintain a record of which clients were introduced by whom

Exceptions

  • Prior written consent from the introducing party
  • Client approaches the other party independently (with proof of unsolicited contact)
  • Publicly available services offered to the general market

Breach consequences: Any breach of non-solicitation terms shall entitle the aggrieved party to (a) immediate termination of this agreement, (b) payment of damages equal to 100% of revenue generated from the solicited client over 24 months, and (c) injunctive relief as appropriate.

11. Duration & Exit

  • Intent is to build long-term relationship if this project succeeds
  • Survival: Sections 9 (Confidentiality) and 10 (Non-Solicitation) survive termination
